These birds are easy to find in pet shops. Senegal parrot are beautiful and fun to play with.

Exercise

Senegal loves to chew. Providing them with wooden perches and rawhide are good ways to exercise their beak.

Provide a cage that is large enough to accomodate lots of toys to keep them entertain while you're at work. Large enough for a swing to swing, ropes and chew toys. Twisted cotton ropes, ladder, and especially swing will keep them busy. A grooming perch is a good idea to keep their nails trimmed.

Senegal parrots are charming, highly trainable and have a great personality. They are very curious but can get frighten easily.
