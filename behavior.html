They are very comical and entertaining. Senegal parrot also have the ability to talk and mimic.

Behavior and Temperament

Senegal parrot strive for attention and becomes very attached to their owner. They have a tendency to become attached to one person in the househould and does not like to inertact with other people.

It's a good idea to give them as much attention as you can. They can be very happy just sitting on your shoulder all day.

They are charming and highly trainable. Very curious creature but can get spook easily.
