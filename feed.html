Fedding and Caring

Senegal parrot generally are not picky eaters. They love table food. You should always have a bowl of parrot seeds and pellets. They also love millets whcih can be hang on top of the cage.

Always make sure to have fresh water inside thier cage. You might need to clean the water bowl serveral times a day to keep it clean and fresh through out the day. Keeping their cage clean daily will also help avoid fungal disease.

It's also a good idea to feed them fresh fruits and vegetables. Chop up some furits or vegetables that you're preparing for yourself and place some of it in their food dish.
